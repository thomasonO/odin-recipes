New England Clam Chowder

This Chowder will Transport you to the shores of New England

This is one of our most popular soups and is a staple on the menu at Van Patton Golf Club

This recipe makes almost 2 gallons so its perfect for holiday parties or for gameday parties

Ingredients

  • 2 sleeves of bacon
  • 1 large white onion
  • 1/2 sleave of celery stalks
  • 3 tbsp of minced garlic
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 2 cups of Sherry wine
  • 1 quart of heavy cream
  • 1 quart of half and half
  • Just under 2 cups of flour
  • 2 51 oz cans of clams
  • 4 large potatos
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Dice bacon and put in a large soup pot on meduim low heat
  2. Whilr bacon is cooking small dice onion,celery and mince garlic
  3. Once bacon is brown add diced onion and celery
  4. Once onion is borowned add minced garlic and cook until fragerant
  5. Add bay leaves
  6. Deglaze pot with the Sherry
  7. Let alcohol cook off and then add flour
  8. Stir in the flour making sure not to burn it
  9. Add the cans of clams and stir
  10. Add heavy cream and half and half
  11. Stir soup and bring to a simmer
  12. While soup is coming up to a simmer peel the potatos
  13. Once peeled small dice the potatos
  14. Once soup is at a simmer put in the potatos
  15. Add salt and pepper to taste
  16. Let soup simmer for 20-30 minutes and make sure potatos are cooked before serving
