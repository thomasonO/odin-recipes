Broccoli Cheddar Soup

A great crowd pleaser for any Occation

This easy to make soup is one of our best sellers at Van Patton Golf Club

This recipe makes almost 2 gallons so its perfect for large gathering and restaurants

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of butter
  • 2 Large white onions
  • 2 tbsp of minced garlic
  • 3 jumbo carrots
    • 3 pounds of Broccoli flowerettes
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 quarts chicken stock
  • 1 quart half and half
  • 3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp onion powder
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Melt butter in large soup pot on medium heat
  2. Medium dice onion and carrots
  3. Once butter is melted add dice veggies
  4. Cook until onions are slightly brown
  5. Once onions are brown add minced garlic and cook until fragerant
  6. Add the flour and stir making to coat all the veg
  7. Cook flour for about 1 mintute then add chicken stock and half and half
  8. Stir soup making sure to scrape the bottom and bring to a simmer
  9. Once soup is at a simmer add broccoli
  10. Let broccoli cook for a minute then add cheddar cheese
  11. Stir until cheese is melted. You want the soup to have a orange color. Add more cheese if needed
  12. Blitz the soup with an imersion bledder until slighty chunky. Make sure you get all the big heads of broccoli
  13. Season with garlic powder, onion powder and salt and pepper to taste
